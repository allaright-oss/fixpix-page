How to Take a Good Photo

1️⃣ Remove any unnecessary items from the frame and use a plain, neutral background (such as a wall or a sheet of paper).
2️⃣ Use soft, natural, or indoor lighting. Avoid harsh shadows and glare.
3️⃣ Make sure the photo is sharp and clear — the object should not be blurry and must be visible.
4️⃣ The entire object should be visible in the frame — no parts cropped out, photographed from the front or above.
